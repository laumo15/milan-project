Why Milan?

Milan: The lifestyle capital of Italy

Milan may not be the official capital of Italy, but it's what's the hype is all about. The whole world of design and fashion gathers here for Salone del Mobile and Milan Fashion Week.

Here there's also one of Europe's major stock exhcanges and the Italian heart of business. Excellent transportation and city services mean that Milan is the most European of all Italian cities. Add to that amazing food, architecture and history, and you have every reason to come visit Milan.
